What We Do

Our Services

Drain Clearing

Blocked or slow drains cleared quickly and thoroughly — from kitchen sinks and floor drains to main sewer lines. We get it flowing again the same day.

Water Lines

Full water line repair and replacement for leaks, low pressure, or aging pipes. Residential supply lines and underground service connections across Greater Vancouver.

Perimeter Drainage / Wet Basement

We excavate, install new drain tile, gravel beds, and waterproofing membrane to keep your foundation dry for the long term.

Video Drain Inspection

Camera inspection down your drain lines identifies root intrusion, pipe collapse, offset joints, or buildup — before we dig, saving you time and money.

Water Jet Drain Flushing

High-pressure water jetting cuts through grease, scale, and debris that drain snakes can't touch — restoring full flow and leaving pipe walls clean.
