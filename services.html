What We Do

Our Services

Drain Clearing

Blocked or slow drains don't have to be a headache. Our team uses professional equipment to clear blockages quickly and thoroughly — from kitchen sinks and floor drains to main sewer lines. We get it flowing again the same day.

Water Lines

Whether you have a leak, low pressure, or aging pipes that need replacing, we handle full water line repair and replacement. We work on both residential supply lines and underground service connections across Greater Vancouver.

Perimeter Drainage / Wet Basement

A wet basement is a sign your perimeter drainage system isn't doing its job. We excavate, install new drain tile, gravel beds, and waterproofing membrane to keep your foundation dry for the long term.

Video Drain Inspection

Before digging, we look. Our camera inspection service sends a waterproof camera down your drain lines to identify exactly what the problem is and where — root intrusion, pipe collapse, offset joints, or buildup — saving you time and money.

Water Jet Drain Flushing

High-pressure water jetting cuts through grease, scale, and debris that drain snakes can't touch. It's the most thorough way to restore full flow to a drain line and it leaves the pipe walls clean.
